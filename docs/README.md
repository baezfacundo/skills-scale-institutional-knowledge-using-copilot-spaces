## OctoAcme Project Management: Overview

OctoAcme's approach to project management is built on clarity of purpose, well-defined roles, and a structured lifecycle that facilitates the delivery of valuable features, services, and integrations. Projects start with a robust initiation stage, requiring a one-pager defining the business need, success metrics, stakeholders, timeline, and resource estimates. Once initiated and approved, teams progress through detailed planning—breaking work into prioritized, estimated increments with clear acceptance criteria, mapping out risks, dependencies, and release plans.

Key roles drive the process: Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, schedules, and risks; Product Managers own the product vision, define measurable objectives, and prioritize the roadmap; Developers design, implement, and test solutions to meet defined criteria. These roles collaborate closely, maintaining strong accountability and a customer-first mindset. Quality assurance is integrated at every phase: automated tests, CI linting, end-to-end smoke tests for critical flows, manual QA, and security scanning are routine parts of the PR workflow, reinforced by clear definitions of done and periodic risk reviews.

Communication is structured but adaptable. The team holds daily standups focusing on progress and blockers, with regular weekly syncs for delivery updates and risk reviews. Stakeholders are kept informed through monthly updates, release announcements, and incident reports when needed. Templates for one-pagers, status reports, risk registers, and release notes standardize information across the organization, ensuring transparency and a single source of truth for decision-making and escalation.

Continuous improvement is a fundamental value. After each sprint or major milestone—and after incidents—teams conduct retrospectives to identify what went well, what to improve, and actionable next steps with assigned owners and due dates. These outcomes are tracked in the project backlog to ensure learnings actively feed back into process evolution. OctoAcme's unified system shapes not only project execution and quality delivery but also psychological safety and iterative learning, accelerating onboarding and organizational resilience.

---

## Guiding Principles

- **Customer-first:** prioritize customer value and usability in every decision.
- **Iterative delivery:** deliver small, testable increments rather than big-bang releases.
- **Clear ownership:** every project has a named PM and PdM accountable for delivery and outcomes.
- **Data-informed decisions:** measure impact and iterate based on evidence.
- **Psychological safety:** encourage feedback, learning, and open communication.
